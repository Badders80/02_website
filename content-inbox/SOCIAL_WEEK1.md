## Content Rules Going Forward

1. Lead with the horse, never the tech
2. The trainer's voice is your voice — quote them not yourself
3. Every post should make someone feel something about racing
4. "Digital syndication" is banned from social. "The stable" or "your horse" instead.
5. Film everything. 5 seconds is enough. I turn 5 seconds into a post.
