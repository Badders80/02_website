Prudentia (NZ): The Winter Campaign

"A gritty, come-from-behind victory at Te Rapa defined a quarter where Prudentia proved her resilience on testing winter turf."

The quarter opened at Tauranga on 14 March with a fifth in Rating 65 company — a useful resumption that set the base for what followed. Four weeks later at Te Rapa, Prudentia broke through with the performance that defined this campaign: from midfield under Masa Hashizume, she produced a gritty come-from-behind win over 1300m on Heavy 9, taking Rating 65 by a short margin.

She was stepped straight into Rating 75 and answered immediately. A close third on debut over 1400m at Te Rapa on 2 May confirmed she belonged at the higher grade. The campaign closed at Tauranga on 27 June on a heavy, sticky surface that did not suit her running style — an honest sixth that underscored what the stable already knew: she wants genuine heavy, not mud.
