Tauranga Return: Prudentia Primed for Saturday

We are pleased to report that Prudentia has made a complete recovery from the mild front-foot soreness that interrupted her campaign two weeks ago. Under the expert guidance of Lance O'Sullivan and Andrew Scott at Wexford Stables, her fitness was seamlessly maintained through a dedicated swimming regimen before she successfully transitioned back to track gallops. To ensure her comfort, the farrier has fitted protective pads to both front feet.

Her recent work has been highly encouraging. On Monday morning, she cantered on the sand track displaying a fluid, comfortable, and sound stride. This was backed up by a sharp grass-track gallop alongside stablemate Ribkraka, where both horses worked through their lines with excellent pace and pullup clean. The trainers are confident she has not lost any of her core racing conditioning and is entering Saturday's race in top physical order.
