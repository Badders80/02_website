  Investor Update — Prudentia

Back to Maiden Ground

She broke her maiden at Tauranga. This Saturday she returns to the same track — recovered, sharp in her work, and accepted for Race 7 from Barrier 1. Can she do it again?

Prudentia with Masa Hashizume — back together for Saturday’s BM75 at Tauranga

A minor front-foot soreness in early June prompted a tactical withdrawal from her 13 June engagement — the right call to protect the longer campaign. Lance O'Sullivan and Andrew Scott kept her fit through swimming before bringing her back to trackwork with protective pads on both front feet. Her movement is fluid again, and recent work — including a partnered grass gallop with stablemate Ribkraka — has her pulling up clean and sharp.

 
“She’s won here, she thrives on heavy footing, and carrying the 54kg minimum gives her a massive tactical edge on a testing winter track.”
— Andrew Scott, Wexford Stables

She lines up in Race 7 (BM75) over 1400m at 4:21 PM on Saturday, 27 June. Barrier 1, minimum weight 54.0kg, and Masa Hashizume is booked to ride. Ribkraka (Barrier 6) also takes her place for Wexford in the same race.
