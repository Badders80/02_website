Feature performance

Prudentia delivered a standout performance at Te Rapa on 17 April 2026, winning by a commanding margin and collecting $20,125 NZD in stakes. This result represented 80% of total campaign earnings and confirmed her competitiveness at the current class level.

Class progression

Following the Te Rapa win, Prudentia has been reassessed and is now competing at a higher rating band. Her two subsequent starts at the elevated level returned placings, demonstrating she is competitive in stronger company. The campaign trajectory supports continued upside potential.

Outlook

Prudentia completed her Tauranga start on 27 June (6th, no stakes) on testing ground. The stable is confident in her current form and fitness heading into winter racing. Performance updates continue between quarterly reports.
