About this report

This quarterly investor report is issued by Evolution Stables in partnership with Tokinvest DMCC for holders of Prudentia (NZ) digital tokens (Asset ID 10). It forms part of the ongoing reporting obligations under the syndication agreement and is published each calendar quarter.

The report covers race performance, prize money earned, the calculation of investor returns through the syndication structure, and the distribution carried out by Tokinvest. Stakes earned in the final month of the quarter are carried forward to the next distribution cycle, as settlement from New Zealand Thoroughbred Racing occurs after the distribution date.

This document is confidential and intended solely for Prudentia token holders. It is co-published by Evolution Stables, an authorised NZTR syndicator, and Tokinvest DMCC, licensed by the Dubai Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA).

The horse

Prudentia is a 4-year-old bay mare by Proisir (AUS) out of Little Bit Irish (NZ), bred by Goldeye Trust and trained by Lance O'Sullivan & Andrew Scott at Wexford Stables, Matamata. She races in the Bloodstock Achieving Excellence I'm Pru Syndicate (Manager: K Bax) and has established a preference for 1300–1600m on heavy ground, with both career wins coming on Heavy9–10 tracks.

Feature performance

The standout came at Te Rapa on 17 April — a decisive Rating 65 win over 1300m on Heavy9, claiming $20,125 in stakes and earning a 5-point rating rise. This single result drove 80% of the quarter's distributable earnings.

Key highlights & achievements

Class progression

Graduated from BM65 to BM75 and placed third on debut at the higher grade — competitive immediately against stronger fields.

Feature performance

Decisive Te Rapa victory on 17 April under Masa Hashizume — the standout run of the quarter and the performance that drove this distribution.

Consistency

Earned a placing or finish in the frame in three of five starts. Handles varying going, including heavy and soft tracks.

Milestone

First full investor distribution cycle completed — from racecourse performance through to platform payout for all token holders.

Looking ahead

The stable's focus over the coming months: consolidate at Rating 75, target 1400m–1600m races that suit her running style, and maintain fitness through the winter programme. Evolution Stables will continue performance updates between quarterly reports. Your next full financial summary and distribution is expected in Q3 2026.
